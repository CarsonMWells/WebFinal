Behind the Collaboration
 Abbie reached out to Phillip Bowles at Selah Clothing Co, with her new single, There Goes That Man . The song emphasizes that trusting God brings healing to brokenness, transforms past pain, and continually works for the singer’s renewal and hope. The team at Selah jumped at the opportunity, excited to work with Abbie and create a collection that blended style with an emphasis on the proclamation of the gospel.
